Vrinda Store Data Analysis Dashboard — MS Excel
The Vrinda Store MS Excel Dashboard offers a comprehensive analytical view of the store's e-commerce sales operations across India. Built on a rich dataset capturing thousands of transactions, the dashboard transforms raw order-level data into meaningful, decision-ready insights.
What the Dashboard Covers:

Sales & Revenue Trends — Tracks monthly and periodic revenue fluctuations, helping identify peak seasons, high-performing periods, and revenue dips that require strategic attention.
Customer Demographics — Breaks down purchases by gender and age group, revealing that women form the dominant buyer segment, enabling more targeted marketing and product curation.
Order Status Analysis — Monitors the share of Delivered, Cancelled, Returned, and Refunded orders across channels, providing a clear picture of fulfillment efficiency and post-purchase behavior.
Channel Performance — Evaluates sales contribution across platforms like Amazon, Myntra, Flipkart, Ajio, Meesho, Nalli, and Others — identifying top-performing and underperforming channels.
Category & Product Insights — Analyzes demand across categories such as Kurtas, Sets, Sarees, Tops, and Western Dresses, along with size distribution trends to guide inventory planning.
Geographic Reach — Maps orders across Indian states and cities, spotlighting high-demand regions like Maharashtra, Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh, and Delhi for region-specific strategies.

This dashboard equips business stakeholders with a 360° view of store performance — driving smarter inventory management, optimized channel investment, and data-backed growth decisions.
